About the Developer

Eva is a Full-Stack web developer based in Charlotte, NC with a passion for technology and coding. To pursue a career in tech, Eva took a proactive approach and enrolled in a bootcamp to learn programming. The bootcamp focused on foundational courses in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Through the completion of the bootcamp, Eva gained hands-on experience in both front-end and back-end development. In addition to coding, Eva enjoys pickleball on the weekends and hiking the beautiful trails around Charlotte. Eva is currently seeking an entry-level position where she can apply her skills and continue to grow as a developer. She is particularly excited about joining a dynamic team that values continuous learning and innovation.
